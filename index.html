About me.

Research Fellow

Graduated in Energy and Nuclear Engineering in October 2020 I worked as grant researcher of BAEDA Lab at the Department of Energy of Politecnico di Torino. Currently enrolled in PhD program dealing with advanced analysis of building related data through data mining and machine learning algorithms.

Research Fellow

I am currently involved in the development of Energy Management and Information Systems (EMIS) embedded into an Energy Analytics Dashboard implementing machine learning algorithms to support buildings energy management in daily operation and facilitate decision making towards energy savings procedures.

Masters Degree

My final work proposes an Energy Information System tool that implements an innovative top-down Anomaly Detection and Diagnostics methodology. This methodology was conceived for working in streaming allowing an automatic detection of energy consumption anomalies at whole building level and a diagnosis to evaluate the sub-load responsible for the anomalies detected..
