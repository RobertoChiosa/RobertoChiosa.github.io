Objectives

What do I want to pursue during my PhD

Real world case studies

Define and drive adoption of novel robust EMIS frameworks through real-world case studies (e.g., Politecnico di Torino Campus), assessing the effectiveness in the energy waste reduction at different scale, from single building to district scale.

Reduce energy wastes

Integrate semi-supervised methods and domain knowledge into the building energy decision-making process, supporting stakeholders to take energy-wise policies through innovative, informative and interactive smart-platforms.

Anomaly detection

Investigate how advanced control systems can effectively interact with EMIS by identifying faults and anomalies from whole building-level (i.e., meter-level) to single energy system level (e.g., heating cooling and ventilating systems).

Smart platforms

Develop open Web-based building energy data analytics applications for the characterization of indoor environmental comfort conditions, by integrating the user feedback (i.e., human in the loop) to improve the energy awareness in the built environment.
